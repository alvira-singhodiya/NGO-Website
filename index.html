About EduRise Foundation

EduRise Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education to underprivileged children around the world. Founded in 2021, we've been working tirelessly to bridge the education gap and create opportunities for children from disadvantaged backgrounds.

Our mission is simple: to ensure that every child, regardless of their socioeconomic status, has access to quality education and the resources they need to succeed in life.

Explore Our Programs

Our Programs

We offer a range of programs designed to provide holistic education and support to underprivileged children.

School Establishment

We build and renovate schools in underserved communities, ensuring children have a safe environment for learning.

Scholarship Program

We provide scholarships to talented but underprivileged children, covering their educational expenses.

Digital Literacy

We provide computer labs and digital literacy programs to prepare children for the digital age.
